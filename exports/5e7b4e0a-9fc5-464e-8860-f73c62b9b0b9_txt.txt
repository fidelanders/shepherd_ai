SUMMARY
------------------------------------------------------------
The sermon opens John 3:1–21 through Nicodemus's night-time visit, tracing the necessity of new birth, the provision through the Son lifted up, and the scope of God's love in John 3:16–17. Salvation is not earned but received through trust in the given Son.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS
------------------------------------------------------------
1. What does it mean to be 'born of the Spirit' in your daily experience?
2. How does John 3:16 read differently now than when you first heard it?
3. Where have you been approaching Jesus in the dark, like Nicodemus?
4. What does it mean that God sent his Son to save, not to condemn (v.17)?
5. How would you explain the new birth to someone hearing this for the first time?

NEWSLETTER BLURB
------------------------------------------------------------
This Sunday we walked slowly through John 3:16, framed by Nicodemus's encounter with Jesus. We were reminded that the new birth is not something we achieve — it is something God does in us.
